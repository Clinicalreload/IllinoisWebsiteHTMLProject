Norridge

Norridge is not very big, actually considered an Island surronded on every side by Chicago. According to Google population is a low 14,572 people. Norridge was incorporated in 1948, mainly being an all grassland suburban area. Norridge is located in the notheastern part of Illinois and the residents make above average income here. There is only 1 highschool in the area called Ridgewood whos name came from a combination of the 2 towns of Norridge and Harwood Heights. Norridge has remained untouched by Chicago for over 75 years.
