Palatine

Palatine subdivision is home to the Race Track that the Chicago Bears purchased in 2023 to potentially build their new stadium at. Palatine according to Google has a population of 68,925 people. Palatine is currently my home town for the last 3 years. There are many activites to do in Palatine to maintain an active lifestyle. Palatine is classified as a suburban city with the residents making above average income. There are numerous ways to travel around in Palatine, major highways, expressways and train stations allowing for access to anywhere within Illinois itself. The Palatine highschool basketball team as of March 12th, 2024 is currently in the state championship game which if they win would be a 1st in Palatine history.

Food

You cannot come to Palatine without getting yourself food from the local mom and pop places. Pizza is by far my favorite food, especially from Napoli. You cannot go wrong with a spunky dunker donuts(spin off dunkin donut), or have a realxing drink and listen to live music at Jimmy D's.
