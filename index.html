Welcome to Illinois

The website you are viewing is for the state of Illinois. For more information outside of this site about Illinois feel free to visitwww.illinois.gov.

Illinois became the 21st state of the United States of America on December 3, 1818. Illinois is surrounded by 6 other states which are: Michigan, Wisconsin, Ohio, Kentucky, Missouri and Iowa. Illinois has a population of 12.81 million people. Illinois is located in the Midwest Region of the United States of America.
